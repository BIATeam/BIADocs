# Prerequisite

## Environment
* [.NET 9](https://dotnet.microsoft.com/en-us/download/dotnet/9.0)
* [Visual Studio](https://visualstudio.microsoft.com/fr/)

## Knowledge to have for DotNet:
* [C#](https://docs.microsoft.com/fr-fr/dotnet/csharp/)
* [.NET Core](https://docs.microsoft.com/fr-fr/dotnet/core/)

## Knowledge to have for Angular:
* [Angular](https://angular.io/)
* [RXJS](https://www.learnrxjs.io/)
* [NGRX](https://ngrx.io/)
* [PrimeNG component list](https://www.primefaces.org/primeng/setup)
* [angular/flex-layout](https://github.com/angular/flex-layout/wiki)
